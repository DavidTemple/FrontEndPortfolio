What Am I?

I am a Computer Science and Information Technology Major with a minor in Graphic Design. I went to highschool in 2 different states: Atlanta , Georgia and Battle Creek , Michigan and since graducating I have continued my education at Graceland University located in Lamoni ,Iowa. Since I was young I have had a growing fascination with technology (notably video games) and have always wanted to figure out the logic behind the magic. Graceland has allowed me to do so and for that I am grateful. However if it wasnt for basketball, another major influence on my life I wouldnt have had the opportunity to do so. I was recruited to Graceland senior year of highschool and offered a scholarship to play the sport that I loved. Since accepting my offer I have worked rigorously to pursue the education that I have longed for since a child while juggling the many challenges of being a collegiate athlete.
